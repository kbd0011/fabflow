How to read this

X-factor (cycle time ÷ raw process time) is the canonical fab flow metric: 1.0 is a perfectly flowing fab, 3+ signals heavy queueing. Cutting X-factor without adding capacity is "free" cycle-time reduction. On-time delivery is the fraction of lots finishing by their due date. The improvement levers here are the dispatching rule (how the next lot is chosen at each tool) and capacity at the bottleneck.
