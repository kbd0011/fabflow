# References

These are the standard sources behind the methods used here.

- **Hopp, W. & Spearman, M.** *Factory Physics* (3rd ed.). The canonical treatment of
  WIP, cycle time, throughput, Little's Law, the relationship between variability and
  queueing, and CONWIP.
- **Little, J. D. C.** "A Proof for the Queuing Formula: L = λW" (*Operations Research*,
  1961). The WIP / throughput / cycle-time identity used as a cross-check.
- **Uzsoy, R., Lee, C.-Y. & Martin-Vega, L.** "A Review of Production Planning and
  Scheduling Models in the Semiconductor Industry." A survey of fab scheduling and
  dispatching, including re-entrant flow.
- **Panwalkar, S. & Iskander, W.** "A Survey of Scheduling Rules" (*Operations Research*,
  1977). Origin and comparison of dispatching rules (FIFO, SPT, EDD, critical ratio,
  least slack).
- **SimPy** discrete-event simulation framework — https://simpy.readthedocs.io
- **DuckDB** in-process analytical database — https://duckdb.org

The fab parameters (work centers, tool counts, route lengths, MTBF/MTTR) are synthetic
and chosen for illustrative realism, not taken from any specific fab.
